## RECOMMENDED SPLIT STRATEGY

**Content Sections:**

   - Skiadi (brief description, link to hiking guide)

   - Prassa (Featured)

7. **Local Cuisine Snapshot**

8. **Where to Stay** (CONDENSED)

9. **Practical Essentials**

10. **FAQ (5-6 Quick Questions)**
    - Is Kimolos worth visiting?

    - Best time to visit?

## DUPLICATE CONTENT MITIGATION

**Content Overlap Minimization:**
- `/islands/kimolos`: 40-50% different content (quick facts, features, CTAs)
- `/guides/kimolos`: 100% unique detailed content

**When Both Cover Same Topic:**
- `/islands/kimolos`: Topic sentence + essential points (300-500 words)
- `/guides/kimolos`: Full deep dive (800-2,000+ words)
- Use different examples, angles, and context

**Example - "Ferry Information":**

`/islands/kimolos`:
> "Kimolos is accessible only by ferry from mainland Greece. The quickest route is from Milos (25-50 minutes), while ferries from Athens take 5-7 hours. [Book ferries now] → `/ferries`"

`/guides/kimolos`:
> Exhaustive section with all ferry operators, pricing tables, timetables by season, booking strategies, day-by-day options, and detailed route comparisons.

## WHY THIS STRUCTURE WORKS

### For Users:
✓ **Hub page** answers "Should I visit?" quickly (2-3 min read)
✓ **Guide page** answers "How do I plan?" comprehensively (10-15 min read)
✓ Clear pathways to conversion (ferries, hotels, flights)
✓ No confusion or redundant information

### For SEO:
✓ 2x content footprint = more keyword opportunities
✓ Hub ranks for broad, high-volume terms
✓ Guide ranks for specific, long-tail terms
✓ Internal links distribute authority strategically
✓ No duplicate content penalties
✓ Multiple entry points for searchers with different intent

### For Conversions:
✓ Hub drives awareness & interest
✓ Guide drives detailed research & booking decisions
✓ Multiple call-to-action points at optimal moments
✓ Ferry/hotel/flight pages get qualified traffic
✓ Better user journey = higher conversion rates

### For Maintenance:
✓ Updates to one page don't require synchronizing another
✓ Clear ownership of content sections
✓ Easier to expand one page without affecting other
✓ Scalable to other islands (same structure applies)
